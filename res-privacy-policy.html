Random Episode Selector : Privacy Policy



Thank you so much for using Random Episode Selector!


I am committed to maintaining your trust and protecting your privacy.


Local Data Processing
The inputs you enter—such as the maximum number of seasons and the maximum number of episodes per season—are processed entirely on your device to generate your randomized result.<\br>

This data is never transmitted to or stored on any external servers.


Advertising & Third-Party Services
To support the ongoing development of this app, advertisements are served through Google AdMob.

• Privacy First: This app does not track your behavior across other companies' apps or websites. We intentionally serve non-personalized ads to respect your privacy.

• Technical Data Collection: While ads are non-personalized, the Google AdMob SDK may automatically process basic non-personal information, such as your device type, diagnostic data, and anonymous identifiers. This data is used strictly to prevent fraud, limit ad repetition, and ensure ad delivery stability.


Thank you, and I hope you enjoy my app!


For more details on how Google manages this data, please refer to the official Google Privacy & Terms.
